RONI

Make-more-roni!

Wow, delicious, just wow. How is it done you ask?

Simple.

Ingredients:

  • Cheese
  • Tubes
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps:

  1. Salt and wet the tubes in a hot place.
  2. Grate all the cheese while the salty tubes wetten and hotten.
  3. Unwet the tubes, add the grated cheese to the hot tubes.
  4. Once the cheese has melted, add pepper.
