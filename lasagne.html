LAS-MY-AGNE

Lasagne, Pronounced like "Champagne"

Wow, delicious, just wow. How is it done you ask?

Simple.

Ingredients:

  • Cheese
  • Meats
  • Sheets
  • Heats

Steps:

  1. Mash the cheese and the meats.
  2. Put the mashed cheese and meats between some sheets.
  3. Put the cheesy meaty sheets in some heats.
  4. Eat the heated cheese meat sheets.
